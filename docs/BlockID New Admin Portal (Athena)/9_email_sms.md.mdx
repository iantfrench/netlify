# Email and SMS 

## Overview

Configure your outgoing email and SMS settings by adding or removing your provider account details and gateway information. Email and SMS messages will be sent using the account information specified. 



### Add Email Gateway

Add an email gateway to send emails through your email provider. You will need to provide your account details and outgoing mail server information. 



1. Login in your BlockID admin panel as a tenant or community administrator 

2. Click **Email & SMS** from the Settings menu on the left-hand side of the page 


<br />![image-20220112171554690.png](https://cdn.document360.io/aeae7c88-d2f4-4a9c-bba9-c4f368260d78/Images/Documentation/image-20220112171554690.png){height="" width=""}

3. Click **Add new gateway**

4. Under **Gateway Info** enter a name and select Email for the channel type 

5. Under **Email Provider Settings** enter the outgoing email server details and account information

   <br /><img src="https://cdn.document360.io/aeae7c88-d2f4-4a9c-bba9-c4f368260d78/Images/Documentation/image-20220112171659784.png"  alt="" style="zoom:50%;" />

   

6. Click **Create** located at the bottom-right to save

7. Your new email gateway will now be visible on the **Email and SMS** page. 



### Add SMS Gateway

Add an SMS gateway to send text messages through your SMS provider. You will need to provide your account details and SMS gateway information. 

:::(Warning) (**Supported SMS Providers**)
Twilio, Infobip and Karix are currently supported 
::: 



1. Login in your BlockID admin panel as a tenant or community administrator 

2. Click **Email & SMS** from the Settings menu on the left-hand side of the page 

<br />![image-20220112171606903.png](https://cdn.document360.io/aeae7c88-d2f4-4a9c-bba9-c4f368260d78/Images/Documentation/image-20220112171606903.png){height="" width=""}

3. Click **Add new gateway**

4. Under **Gateway Info** enter a name and select SMS for the channel type 

5. Under **SMS Provider Settings** select your SMS provider and enter the account information and service details

<br/><img src="https://cdn.document360.io/aeae7c88-d2f4-4a9c-bba9-c4f368260d78/Images/Documentation/image-20220112171858292.png" alt="image-20220112171858292" style="zoom:50%;" />

6. Click **Create** located at the bottom-right to save

7. Your new SMS gateway will now be visible on the **Email and SMS** page.



### Delete Email or SMS Gateway



1. Login in your BlockID admin panel as a tenant or community administrator 

2. Click **Email & SMS** from the Settings menu on the left-hand side of the page 

3. Find the gateway you wish to delete and click the **trash icon** to the right of the Gateway Name, under Actions

<br/>![image-20220112171947871.png](https://cdn.document360.io/aeae7c88-d2f4-4a9c-bba9-c4f368260d78/Images/Documentation/image-20220112171947871.png){height="" width=""}

4. Click **Delete** when prompted to remove the gateway
